Mission Statement

The All-Ohio program is dedicated to supporting and impacting youth through athletic, social, and cultural experiences. In hopes that it gives our youth the foundation that leads to successful adulthood.

The All-Ohio staff is committed to the development of its players both on and off the court. Countless years of experience and dedication to youth and the game of basketball make our staff uniquely qaulified to lead our players on the court. Please feel free to contact the coaches directly for more information in regards to tryout information and upcoming events.

About Us

The All-Ohio Summer Basketball Program has been recognized by college coaches and the media as one of the outstanding summer programs around the country. Former All-Ohio player Jared Sullinger is in his 4th NBA season playing a key role with the Boston Celtics. Terry Rozier, who played at the University of Louisville, entered the NBA draft after his sophomore season and was the 16th player taken in the 2015 NBA draft. The All-Ohio basketball program has had a player taken in the last 5 NBA drafts. Trey Burke is playing a key role for the Utah Jazz of the NBA western conference.

The All-Ohio Red 4th grade team was ranked as one of the top five teams in the country. The All-Ohio Red 5th grade team finished in the top eight in the country at the AAU nationals. The All-Ohio Red 8th grade team won several major tournaments and were ranked top five in the country by most scouting publications. The All-Ohio red 10th grade team won the gold division championship at the AAU Super Showcase in Louisville, Kentucky.

Kaleb Wesson, who played for the All-Ohio Red sophomore team, is ranked in the top sixty in the country and committed to play at The Ohio State University after his senior year in high school. The All-Ohio Red junior team won two major summer tournaments – the Best of the South tournament in Suwanee, Georgia and the NY2LA Sports Summer Jam in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Nick Ward and Ibi Watson, who played for the All-Ohio Red junior team, are going to Michigan State and Michigan, respectively, to play in the Big 10 conference.

Big things are expected from former All-Ohio players Nigel Hayes (Wisconsin), Caris Levert (Michigan), Marc Loving (Ohio State), Jae’Sean Tate (Ohio State), and Vince Edwards (Purdue). All are expected to have good college seasons. Former All-Ohio player Keon Johnson, playing for Winthrop University, is one of the exciting little men in college basketball.

The All-Ohio program has been the winning-est AAU program in the country with 9 national championships. Our program has over 50 active players playing college basketball. Over 460 players who played in the All-Ohio basketball program have received scholarships over the last 29 years. Our program has featured the finest basketball events Ohio has to offer.
