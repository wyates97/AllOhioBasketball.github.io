AMERICA'S PREMIER BASKETBALL PROGRAM

Best in The Midwest

The All-Ohio Basketball Program has been hosting AAU basketball events since 1986. All-Ohio has produced more College and Professional basketball players than any program in Ohio or surrounding states. If you aspiration is to play at the next level the obvious choice is to play on one of the All-Ohio teams.

All-Ohio Alumni


The All-Ohio Summer Basketball program has been recognized by college coaches and the media as one of the outstanding summer programs around the country. The All-Ohio program has been the winning-est AAU program in the country with 9 national championships. Our program has over 50 active players playing college basketball. Over 420 players who played in the All-Ohio basketball program have received scholarships over the last 26 years. Our program has featured the finest basketball events Ohio has to offer
